Currency Converter V 0.2.0, Welmo

Currency Converter converts currencies with daily exchange rates updates with the European Central Bank (ECB)
It allows:

  • Multi direct and indirect conversion just clocking on one currency and it calculates the values for all other currencies currencies in the screen.
  • List of available currencies is configurable as the source currency
  • Source for currencies synchronization is BCE. (New sources will be added with new versions
