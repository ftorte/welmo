Currency Converter converts up to all countries currencies worldwide.

It allows:

  • Direct and indirect conversion just clocking on one currency and it calculates the values for all other currencies currencies in the screen.

  • List of available currencies is configurable as the source currency

  • Synchronization can be automatic configurable or manual

  • Source for currencies synchronization can be selected between official available free service worldwide (BCE, CAD)
